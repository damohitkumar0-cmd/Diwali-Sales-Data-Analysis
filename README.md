# Diwali-Sales-Data-Analysis - Python Project
**Project Overview**:-
This project involves an end-to-end Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) of sales data during the Diwali festival. The primary objective is to analyze customer purchasing behavior across different demographics—such as gender, age, state, and occupation—to help businesses improve their customer experience and drive sales growth through data-driven insights.

**Key Objectives**:-
To clean and prepare the raw sales dataset for analysis.
To identify the primary customer segments (Gender and Age groups).
To determine the top-performing states and industries contributing to sales.
To identify the most popular product categories and specific products.

**Technical Stack**:-
Language: Python
Libraries: Pandas (Data Manipulation), NumPy (Numerical Operations), Matplotlib & Seaborn (Data Visualization).

**Data Cleaning & Preprocessing**:-
The project began with a rigorous data cleaning phase to ensure the accuracy of the analysis:
Removing Unnecessary Columns: Dropped blank/redundant columns like Status and unnamed1.
Handling Missing Values: Identified and removed null values to maintain data integrity.
Data Type Conversion: Converted the Amount column from float to integer for better calculations.
Structural Check: Verified the dataset schema using .info() and .describe() to understand distributions.

**Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)**:-
The analysis was divided into several key segments using visualizations like Bar Charts and Count Plots:
Gender Analysis: Discovered that females represent the majority of buyers and have a significantly higher purchasing power compared to males.
Age Group Analysis: Analyzed sales across various age brackets, identifying the most active consumer group.
Geographic Analysis: Identified the top-performing states (Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, and Karnataka) in terms of both order volume and total sales amount.
Occupational & Sector Analysis: Evaluated which professional sectors (IT, Healthcare, and Aviation) contribute most to the revenue.
Product Category Analysis: Identified high-demand categories such as Food, Clothing, and Electronics.

**Key Insights & Conclusion**:-
The analysis revealed a specific "Power Buyer" profile:
Primary Target Audience: Married women aged 26-35 years.
Geographic Focus: Top regions include Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, and Karnataka.
Professional Background: High-spending customers are predominantly from the IT, Healthcare, and Aviation sectors.
Preferred Products: These customers show a strong preference for products in the Food, Clothing, and Electronics categories.

**Potential Business Impact**
By focusing marketing efforts and inventory management on these specific demographics and regions, businesses can optimize their advertising spend and
increase conversion rates during the peak holiday season.
